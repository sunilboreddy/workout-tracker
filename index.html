How to Progress

🎯 Universal Progression Protocol
Step 1: Master the rep range (e.g., 3×8-12)
Week 1: 3×8,7,6 → Week 2: 3×9,8,7 → Week 3: 3×10,9,9

Step 2: Once you hit top range on ALL sets (3×12,12,12)
Add 2-4kg for weights OR advance variation for bodyweight

Step 3: Reset to bottom of range with new weight
Example: 20kg 3×12 → 24kg 3×8,7,7 (build back up)

Golden Rule: Add 1-2 total reps per week

Goblet Squat Progression

Weeks 1-4: Foundation
16kg KB → 4×10-12 reps
Focus: Perfect depth, chest up, knees out
Weeks 5-8: Build Load
20-24kg KB → 4×10-12 reps
Add 2-4kg when you hit 4×12 clean reps
Weeks 9-12: Heavy Loading
28-32kg KB → 4×8-10 reps
OR Double KB front squat (16kg each hand)
Week 13+: Advanced
32kg+ OR tempo variations
3s descent, 1s pause, explosive up

Pull-up Progression

Weeks 1-4: Foundation
Dead hangs: Build to 3×30s
Scapular pull-ups: 5×5 | Band-assisted (heavy): 5×5
Weeks 5-8: Negatives
Negative pull-ups: 5×3 (5s descent)
Band-assisted (medium): 5×6-8 reps
Weeks 9-12: First Pull-up
Band-assisted (light): 5×8-10
Attempt 1-2 unassisted at start of session
Week 13+: Building Volume
Unassisted: 5-8 sets max reps
Aim for 25-30 total volume per session

Push-up Progression

Weeks 1-4
Incline push-ups (high)
4×10-12 reps
Weeks 5-8
Incline push-ups (low)
4×10-15 reps
Weeks 9-12
Floor push-ups (regular)
4×8-12 reps
Week 13+
Advanced variations
Deficit, archer, or diamond push-ups 4×8-12
📝 Practical Example
Week 1: DB Press 10kg: 10,9,8,8 = 35 total
Week 2: DB Press 10kg: 11,10,9,9 = 39 total (+4!)
Week 3: DB Press 10kg: 12,11,10,10 = 43 total
Week 4: DB Press 10kg: 12,12,12,11 = 47 total ✓
Week 5: DB Press 12kg: 8,7,7,6 = 28 total (increased weight!)
